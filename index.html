Manager Effectiveness Survey

This survey assesses the employees’ beliefs about their manager’s effectiveness. This effectiveness is measured through the manager’s values, leadership qualities, communication behaviours, appreciation for diversity, and through capturing the employees’ overall satisfaction with their manager’s performance.
